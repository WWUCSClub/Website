Benefits of the club!



Lectures!

We have lots of informative presentations and lectures on interesting topics. They aren't boring, we promise!

Git Server

One of the biggest benefits is a private git server, hosted on campus and free to use by all club members.

ACM Membership

If you're a part of this club for a quarter, you'll get an ACM Membership. This gives you access to news and the latest findings in the computing world, as well as a host of other benefits.

Mission Statement



The ACM Club is designed to enhance the experience of students interested in computer science at Walla Walla University. The club has three main pillars of focus: enhancing education and developing skills, fostering community, and serving others. Through these pillars the club seeks to enable its members to develop new abilities, form connections, and use their God-given talents in service to others.
